The First Day... 24/02/2022

I couldn't quite believe what I'd heard, or seen. The Chicken Empire, I'd been told, was expansive and glorious, stretching over rolling hills and dark, deep forests. Did they know what they'd had, what they'd lost? The Meerkat Troupe had a monopoly on the chicken kingdom, for sure - the outcome of a war that had occured and devastated the kingdom six years prior -- it's why I was here. There were strange, crude meerkat statues erected supposedly in their honour and they, too, were scattered along the hills -- even in the forests. My blood went cold. I was told that there would be chickens -- chickens aplenty -- as far as the eye could see... but there were none. Were they hiding - biding their time until their numbers were strong enough to rise against the meerkats and take back what was rightfully theirs? A knock at my door. At my feet, a picked flower drowned in orange paint. Could this be a sign? A signal...?
